# Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria

**Inclusion Criteria (IC):**
* **IC1:** The study focuses on the application of Large Language Models (LLMs) in Software Engineering tasks, specifically bug reporting, code review, program repair, or software testing.
* **IC2:** The paper includes a clear empirical evaluation with specific metrics (e.g., reproducibility rate, accuracy, acceptance rate, F1-score).
* **IC3:** Published between January 2020 and June 2026.

**Exclusion Criteria (EC):**
* **EC1:** Papers that are solely tutorials, opinions, surveys (without new empirical data), or posters.
* **EC2:** Studies applying LLMs to domains outside of Software Engineering (e.g., general NLP, medical abstract screening, news summarization).
* **EC3:** Publications not written in English.
