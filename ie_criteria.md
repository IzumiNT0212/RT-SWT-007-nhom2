# Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria

## Inclusion Criteria

A paper is included only if it satisfies all of the following criteria:

- IC1: The paper is written in English.
- IC2: The paper was published from 2018 onward.
- IC3: The paper is related to bug reports, issue reports, GitHub issues, or Jira tickets.
- IC4: The paper is related to LLMs, ChatGPT, GPT, or generative AI in Software Engineering.
- IC5: The paper is related to bug report quality, reproducibility, reproduction, assessment, classification, categorization, or improvement.


## Exclusion Criteria

A paper is excluded if it satisfies any of the following criteria:

- EC1: The paper is a duplicate record with the same title or DOI.
- EC2: The full-text or PDF is not available.
- EC3: The paper is only a poster, extended abstract, slide, blog post, or shorter than 4 pages.
- EC4: The paper is not related to bug reports or issue reports.
- EC5: The paper is not related to LLMs, ChatGPT, GPT, or generative AI.
- EC6: The paper is only a survey/opinion paper without experiment or evaluation.
- EC7: The paper only focuses on general test execution or maintenance and is not related to bug report quality, reproducibility, or assessment.
