# Inclusion / Exclusion Criteria

## Inclusion Criteria (IC)

- **IC1 — Language:** Paper is written in English.
- **IC2 — Timeline:** Published between 2020 and 2025.
- **IC3 — Domain:** Related to bug reports, defect reports, issue reports, bug reproduction, or bug-report analysis.
- **IC4 — Technology:** Related to LLMs, ChatGPT, GPT-4, or AI-assisted bug analysis.
- **IC5 — Empirical evidence:** Contains experiment, evaluation, benchmark, comparison, or empirical results.
- **IC6 — Accessibility:** Full text is available.

## Exclusion Criteria (EC)

- **EC1 — Education only:** The study focuses only on classroom/learning context.
- **EC2 — Security/vulnerability only:** The study focuses only on vulnerability repair or cybersecurity without bug-report analysis.
- **EC3 — Code generation only:** The study evaluates generated code quality only, not bug reports or defect reports.
- **EC4 — Out of scope:** The study is not related to bug reports, defect reports, or reproducibility.
- **EC5 — Duplicate:** The record is duplicated.
- **EC6 — No full text:** Full text is unavailable.
